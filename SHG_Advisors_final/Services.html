Our Experience & Approach

Our Work & Impact

With over 25 years of experience, SHG Advisors works with entities both in the private and public sectors to improve the lives of people in our communities. We are especially excited to focus on systemic change within the areas of behavioral health, affordable housing, and gun violence prevention. However, we are always open to new ideas and suggestions about how we can work together.

For instance, SHG Advisors was engaged by an organization that was quickly gaining a strong reputation (and thus the attention of a lot of funders) to create a quantitative tool to use for programmatic, financial, and operational decision-making. Together, we created a tool that allowed the organization to make smart decisions and remain focused on its vision to support inner-city youth.

Our Services

Evaluation & Learning

We design evaluation systems that help organizations understand what’s working, where to improve, and how to scale impact. Through qualitative and quantitative methods, we create learning tools that strengthen programs and guide future investments.

Community Engagement

We bring together residents, leaders, and partners to shape solutions collaboratively. Our engagement processes prioritize trust, inclusivity, and clear communication, resulting in stronger relationships and better outcomes for communities.

Organizational Development

Building stronger teams and more effective organizations. We support leadership development, change management, and internal capacity building to ensure teams can adapt, grow, and deliver on their mission.
