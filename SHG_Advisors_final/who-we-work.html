Who We Are

Who We Are

SHG Advisors is a boutique consulting firm deeply committed to working with people who want to improve our communities.

We help you balance strategy and execution. We bring your partners together and collectively develop solutions. We analyze data with you to inform your decisions. We provide you with what you need to arm you with the knowledge and capabilities to sustain your growth and progress.

We have worked with almost 40 entities that are invested in helping our communities thrive (the majority of whom are repeat clients), and 91% of our partners have expanded their impact based on our engagement. We are invested in your long-term success.

Our Leadership

Summer Gathercole

Managing Director of SHG Advisors

Excels at doing things that people think are impossible: She recently led Governor Polis’ initiative to reform Colorado’s behavioral health system. Many people said it couldn’t be done because there has been a myriad of efforts to reform Colorado’s system over the past two decades.

But we did it! Summer and the Behavioral Health Task Force, comprised of 120 members, delivered our Blueprint to Governor Polis in September 2020 and he immediately asked us to start implementation.

The recommendations are currently being executed and Colorado is well positioned to be the state in the U.S. that has a comprehensive behavioral health system for all people.
