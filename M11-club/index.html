Frequently Asked Questions

Good to Know.

How do the cash draws work?

Every member gets a free entry into our $10,000 monthly cash draw. Winners are drawn live on Instagram so the whole club can watch. No extra entries to buy — it's included with your membership.

Which states can join?

M11 Club is open to residents across all Australian states and territories. As long as you're 18+ and based in Australia, you're eligible to join and participate in all draws and savings.

What's the 3-month membership?

Our 3-month membership gives you full access to all member-only deals, fuel savings, grocery discounts, and three entries into the monthly $10,000 draw — one for each month of your membership.

How do the savings work?

Once you're a member, you get access to exclusive discounts across 4,000+ brands including groceries, fuel, and everyday essentials. Savings are applied automatically when you shop through the club portal.

How are winners paid?

Winners receive their cash directly via bank transfer within 3–5 business days of the draw. All draws are conducted live and transparently so every member can verify the result.
