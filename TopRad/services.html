Imaging Modalities We Support:

TopRad supports a comprehensive range of imaging modalities with tailored advanced Teleradiology services, including:

    • X-ray – Digital, CR, portable, fluoroscopy, and intraoperative studies
    • Nanox Tomo – Next-generation tomographic X-ray technology, extending traditional radiography to provide enhanced detail and coverage across chest, MSK, spine, and general imaging applications
    • CT – Multislice, contrast/non-contrast, CTA/CTV, perfusion, and advanced 3D reconstructions
    • MRI – Neuro, spine, MSK, abdomen, pelvis, cardiac, breast, and whole-body protocols
    • Ultrasound – General, OB/GYN, vascular, MSK, point-of-care, and Doppler
    • Mammography – Screening, diagnostic, and Tomosynthesis (3D mammography)
    • Nuclear Medicine & PET/CT – SPECT, PET/CT, cardiac stress, bone scans, and oncology staging
    • Interventional Radiology Support – Imaging guidance and interpretation for procedures and post-procedure follow-up
    • Specialty & Advanced Imaging – DEXA bone density, pediatric imaging, and hybrid modalities
