Clinical & Quality Assurance (QA)

Who are the radiologists reading our cases?

All TopRad interpretations are performed by ABR-certified (or board-eligible) radiologists based exclusively in the United States. Our doctors are fellowship-trained in sub-specialties including Neuroradiology, MSK, Body, and Cardiac Imaging.

What are your Turnaround Time (TAT) guarantees?

Routine studies are completed within 2–4 hours, STAT within 30–60 minutes, and critical findings are communicated immediately upon identification.

How do you handle Quality Assurance and Peer Review?

Our QA program includes structured peer review, discrepancy tracking, and monthly performance reporting aligned with ACR standards.

How are critical findings communicated?

Critical findings are communicated directly to the ordering provider via phone within minutes of identification, followed by documentation in the final report.

Technical & IT Integration

What are the hardware requirements for my facility?

Minimal hardware is required. A standard workstation with internet connectivity and a DICOM-compatible modality is sufficient to get started with TopRad.

Do you integrate with my existing PACS and EMR?

Yes. TopRad integrates with most major PACS and EMR systems via HL7 and DICOM standards. Our IT team handles the full integration process at no additional cost.

How is data security and HIPAA compliance managed?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Our infrastructure is fully HIPAA-compliant and we sign a BAA with every partner facility prior to onboarding.
